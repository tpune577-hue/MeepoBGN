# MeepoBGN

## What
Mobile web tool for Thailand Board Game Awards (BGN) — upload a face photo, AI generates a chibi Meepo head sticker for physical Meepo figures.

## Who
Board game fans at events or home, casual creators, bright ambient light, playful celebratory mood.

## Register
product (committed brand identity from BGN Awards visual system)

## Brand cues
- BGN pink meeple puzzle-piece mascot
- Warm near-white surfaces, pink as primary action color
- Cherry blossom accents from awards campaign art
